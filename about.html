The Bampadde Muchomo Hub gets it's name from the late Joseph Bampadde. It was established on the 7th of August 2020 and for the few years it has been operating, it has been and still is the no.1 muchomo joint in all of Kampala. It was started by Monica Nalugya Kasirye Bampadde who first share her signature home cooked dish, grilled pork, and so far it is the best selling item on the menu. We are more than just employees and customers at the muchomo hub, we are a family and we are in it for the long run with our commitment to serve the best dishes all around Kampala and Uganda at large.
